Welcome to SecretVault!
Do you have trouble with remembering your password? Or do you have a lot of passwords to remember but you keep the same password to all your logins just so it is easy for you to remember them? Or do you still save your passwords in the notes app on your phone? Well, SecretVault is the solution for all your needs. It will help you store all your credentials, secret keys, secret notes, etc. Click here to learn more about how to store your logins.
SecretVault is made for everyone! Students can use it to store a code that they purchase to use a service or access a book for their class. A full time employee can use it to store his/her credentials of daily needs such as banking accounts, billing accounts, entertainment accounts, and many more. This platform is designed where it does not restrict any age group from using it.
The main goal of this platform is for the users to experience peace of mind while their secrets are stored safely. Users only need to remember one username and one password for this platform and the rest can be saved inside which is not accessible by anyone but the user.
This platform not only solves the problem of remembering multiple passwords but it allows the users to not duplicate their credentials which prevents an unhealthy habit of re-using the same password for all logins.
